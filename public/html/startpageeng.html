Discover the wonders of banking at OCBC Bank

As a forward-thinking bank, OCBC offers a wide range of financial services, including personal and business banking, wealth management, and investment solutions, designed to help customers achieve their financial goals. With a strong foundation rooted in Southeast Asia, OCBC has expanded its reach globally, serving customers with the same values of integrity, prudence, and foresight that have guided it for over 90 years.

Who Are We?

OCBC is the longest established Singapore bank, formed in 1932 from the merger of three local banks, the oldest of which was founded in 1912. Since our incorporation, we have been enabling people and communities to realise their aspirations.
